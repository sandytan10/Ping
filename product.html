How to Use Ping!

To access PING the number you have to text is 646-768-8479 and by a simple reply of “Subscribe” that will allow you to be subscribed and notified if there is anything happening near your location. This automatic number system will help alert people for preparations due to severe weather conditions.To stop receiving messages just type in "Stop" To not receive messages and to continue start receiving just type in "Start" to continue getting the messages.
